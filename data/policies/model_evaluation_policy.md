# Model Evaluation Policy

## EVALUATION_001 Evaluation dataset and regression testing
Category: evaluation
Tags: evaluation, regression testing, dataset, model quality, monitoring
AI systems used in operational workflows require documented evaluation datasets, acceptance metrics, regression tests, and review of known failure modes before production approval.

## EVALUATION_002 Bias and fairness testing
Category: evaluation
Tags: evaluation, bias, fairness, protected groups, employment, lending
Systems used for employment, credit, healthcare, or other consequential decisions should include bias and fairness testing appropriate to the use case and available data.
