# Internal AI Policy

## HUMAN_OVERSIGHT_001 Human oversight for high-impact AI systems
Category: human oversight
Tags: human oversight, high-impact ai, reviewer guidance, escalation, override
High-impact AI systems must have a documented human oversight procedure. Reviewers must understand system limitations, escalation paths, and override procedures before recommendations are used in consequential decisions.

## TRANSPARENCY_001 User transparency for AI-assisted decisions
Category: transparency
Tags: transparency, notice, ai-assisted decisions, human review, candidates, customers
Teams must document when AI materially influences a user, customer, employee, or candidate workflow. Notices and reviewer guidance should describe the role of the AI system and the availability of human review.
