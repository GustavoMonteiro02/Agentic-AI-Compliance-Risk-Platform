# EU AI Act Summary

## HUMAN_OVERSIGHT_EUAI Employment and human oversight
Category: human oversight
AI systems used in employment-related contexts may require stronger risk management, human oversight, documentation, logging, transparency, and post-market monitoring depending on their role and impact.

## AUDIT_LOGGING_EUAI Technical documentation and logging
Category: audit logging
High-impact AI systems should maintain technical documentation and logs sufficient to support traceability, monitoring, and audit review.
