# GDPR Summary

## DATA_PROTECTION_GDPR Personal data governance
Category: data protection
Jurisdiction: EU
Document Type: regulation
Authority: European Union
Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2016/679/oj
Effective Date: 2018-05-25
Tags: gdpr, personal data, privacy, lawful basis, minimization, retention
Systems processing personal data should document purpose, lawful basis assessment ownership, minimization, retention, data subject considerations, and appropriate technical and organizational controls.
